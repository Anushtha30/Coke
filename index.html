COKE

    Coca-Cola, often referred to simply as Coke, is a globally popular carbonated soft drink produced by The Coca-Cola Company. Introduced in 1886, it is known for its distinct flavor, which combines sweet and tangy notes with a hint of vanilla and caramel. The beverage is widely recognized by its red and white logo and iconic glass bottle design. Coca-Cola is available in various formulations, including diet and zero-sugar versions, and is enjoyed by millions of people around the world as a refreshing beverage.
